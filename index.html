Bio

I am a Developer, Engineer, and Leader with over a decade of experience working for financial institutions, such as Wells Fargo Bank and Charles Schwab. I have built, enhanced and maintained applications. I have raised and documented standards, policies and processes. I am trained and practiced in servant leadership.

When I am not thinking of or implementing technical solutions to better the world around me, I can often be found swimming in the local community pool, spending time with family...playing or watching soccer, jigsaw puzzling, binging Netflix or DisneyPlus, hiking trails throughout the western United States, or just chilling in the backyard.

I strongly believe in giving back to the community by donating my time and efforts to those in need. I provide technical advice to the I-TESOL group in keeping up their website and hosting technical conferences. I have taught basic English language skills to refugees trained and employed at the LDS Humanitarian Center.
